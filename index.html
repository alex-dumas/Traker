Our company is TitanSoft Labs, and our philosophy is:

We want to make you smile!

We want the brilliantly designed interfaces and functionality of our apps, and the fun and interesting plots, and game play in our games, to make you smile!

What is this, 'Traker' app?

Its name is derived from Tracker, and it is a collaboration app, eventually we hope to make it encompass everything you would need to collaborate with other coders, including a chat part, a ticket assignment part, and more!
